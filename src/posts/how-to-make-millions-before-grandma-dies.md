## Plot summary 
Mengju Saejiu lives in Bangkok’s Chinatown and wakes up before dawn every morning to sell congee. When she’s diagnosed with cancer, her three children barely conceal their interest in inheriting her house. To everyone’s surprise, her grandson M, a college dropout and video game streamer, ends up spending the most time with her. He moves into her house, learns to make congee, and takes her to chemotherapy appointments. Of course, he’s interested in his inheritance, too.
